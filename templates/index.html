Model Reference Guide

Supported cotton leaf diseases & corresponding primary deficiency signatures.

Healthy Leaf

No Deficiency

Uniformly green color, natural veins, clear margins, showing no symptoms of physiological stress.

Bacterial Blight

Potassium (K) Stress

Angular water-soaked lesions that turn dark brown. Often resembles severe potassium deficiency.

Leaf Redding

Phosphorus (P) Stress

Leaf margins turn purplish-red while main veins stay green. Typically flags phosphorus deficiency.

Leaf Variegation

Magnesium (Mg) Stress

Interveinal chlorosis, yellowing spots or white stripes, signaling magnesium uptake deficiency.
